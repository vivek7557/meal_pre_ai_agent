## 4. Preparation Instructions

### Batch Preparation Tips:
1. **Wash and chop vegetables** at the beginning of your prep session
2. **Cook grains and legumes** in bulk for multiple meals
3. **Prep proteins** separately to maintain freshness

### Meal-Specific Instructions:

#### Greek Yogurt Parfait
1. Layer 1/2 cup Greek yogurt with 1/4 cup berries and 1 tbsp almonds
2. Drizzle with 1 tsp honey
3. Store in airtight containers for up to 2 days

#### Mediterranean Chickpea Salad
1. Drain and rinse chickpeas
2. Dice cucumber and tomatoes, slice red onion thin
3. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
4. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for dressing
5. Toss salad with dressing and store in refrigerator

#### Zucchini Noodles with Pesto
1. Spiralize zucchini into noodles (or buy pre-spiralized)
2. Roast vegetables: toss cubed eggplant, peppers, and tomatoes with olive oil, roast at 400Â°F for 20 minutes
3. Make pesto: blend basil, pine nuts, garlic, olive oil, and salt
4. Combine zucchini noodles with roasted vegetables and pesto
5. Serve immediately or store separately (zucchini releases water)

#### Hummus with Veggies
1. Cut vegetables into sticks
2. Serve with 2-3 tbsp hummus per serving
3. Store vegetables in water to maintain crispness

#### Stuffed Avocado
1. Cut avocado in half, remove pit
2. Dice tomatoes and onion, mix with crumbled feta and herbs
3. Fill avocado halves with mixture
4. Squeeze lemon juice on top to prevent browning

### Storage Guidelines:
- Salads: 3-4 days in refrigerator
- Cooked vegetables: 4-5 days in refrigerator
- Prepared proteins: 3-4 days in refrigerator
- Fresh fruits: 2-3 days in refrigerator
